Working at an educational "institution":http://www.glam.ac.uk myself, I find this an interesting context for standards and producers attitudes to web development. These bodies often have direct obligations to provide services to well defined populations.

There is often a different culture to a commercial one, and particularly on accessibility there is a desire to get badges to demonstrate compliance.
